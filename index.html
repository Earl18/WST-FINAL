    Our History

    Nestled in the heart of downtown, The Brew Haven has been a beloved fixture in the community for over three decades. Our story begins in 1985 when founder, Christian Jacob Menancillo, a passionate coffee enthusiast, embarked on a mission to bring exceptional coffee to the bustling streets of our city.

    Christian's journey began with a small corner shop adorned with cozy armchairs and the comforting aroma of freshly brewed coffee. With dedication and a commitment to quality, The Brew Haven quickly became a haven for locals seeking refuge from the daily grind.

    Throughout the years, The Brew Haven evolved, adapting to changing tastes and trends while staying true to its roots. In 1998, Christian's trusted friend, Earl, joined the team, bringing with him a flair for innovation and a deep respect for tradition.
